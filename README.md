# **The Mystery of Eldenwood Manor**



#### **Game overview:**

###### This is a detective mystery game where you take on the role of a Detective investigating the suspicious murder of Evan Eledenwood, head of the wealthy Eldenwood family.

###### During this game you will explore the manor, gather clues from different locations, interrogate the suspects with limited energy and finally identifying the real murderer.

###### This game uses randomness for weapons, clues and murderer selection so manage your energy very carefully to gather maximum information.

#### 

#### **How To Play:**

1. ##### **Starting the Game:**

###### From the main menu, choose one option.

###### 

###### Main Menu Options:

1. ###### How to play - Displays the basic instructions
2. ###### Start Game - Begins the story, shows suspects and locations and generates weapons
3. ###### About us - show information about game's developers
4. ###### Exit Game - Closes the program



##### **2. Controls:**

* ###### Input numbers as instructed in the menu.
* ###### All choices throughout the game are made by entering integer inputs.
* ###### Use logic and deduction to solve the case.



##### **2. Energy Systems:**

* ###### You begin with 100% energy.
* ###### Investigating a location cost 5% energy.
* ###### Interrogating a suspect cost 10% energy.
* ###### If energy reaches 0%, you will be forced to make a guess.

##### 

##### **3. Investigating the locations:**

###### You can explore seven different locations in the manor.

###### Look for the murder weapon which is one of the most important clue in the game in these locations.



##### **4. Interrogating the suspects:**

###### There will be ten suspects each with a unique and strong motive to murder.

###### You have to figure out who is trying to mislead you with lies.



##### **5. Gather clues:**

###### Use clues from both locations and suspects interrogation to make a strong guess.



##### **6. Final Accusation:**

###### In the end, you have to accuse one of the suspect as murderer based on your interrogation and other clues.



#### **Winning the Game:**

###### You win by correctly identifying the murderer using:

1. ###### Weapon found
2. ###### Location clues
3. ###### Motives
4. ###### Logical deduction



#### **Losing the Game:**

###### You lose if:

1. ###### Your energy reaches 0% and without preparation you will be forced to accuse the suspect
2. ###### You accuse the wrong suspect



#### **Tips:**

1. ###### Manage your energy level very carefully.
2. ###### Use weapon clues to reduce the suspects list.
3. ###### Think logically about every word of suspect during interrogation, there can be a connection which lead you to murderer.
